Animes trailers

Here are some trailers for the shows I enjoy watching most:

Inuyasha

Japanese / Romance / shoujo / shonen / action / adventure / supernatural

Its a story of a girl named Kagome who periodically travels back in time to feudal Japan to help a young half-demon recover the shards of a jewel of great power.


My first ever anime. This series hold a special place in my heart. If you are a fan of romance anime do watch this one

One Piece

Japanese / Action Epic / Martial Arts / Shonen

Monkey D. Luffy, a pirate with rubber powers, forms the Straw Hat crew and sails the Grand Line, battling rivals and the World Government on a quest for the legendary treasure One Piece and the title of Pirate King.


This is my current favourite anime, i love to watch their journey

The Promised Neverland

Japanese / Adult Animation / Dark Fantasy / Folk Horror / Psychological Drama

When three gifted kids at an isolated idyllic orphanage discover the secret and sinister purpose they were raised for, they look for a way to escape from their evil caretaker and lead the other children in a risky escape plan.


A series that makes me unable to move on from it more than 3 months

Frieren

Japanese / Adult Animation / Sword and Sorcery / Shonen / Fantasy Epic

Elf mage Frieren and her fellow adventurers have defeated the Demon King and brought peace to the land. But Frieren will long outlive the rest of her former party. How will she come to understand what life means to the people around her?


I love the storyline and also the strong female lead. I love watching series that have a strong female lead.

Demon Slayer

Japanese / Adult Animation / Shonen / Dark Fantasy / Supernatural Fantasy / Action / Shonen / Fantasy Epic

A family is attacked by demons and only two members survive - Tanjiro and his sister Nezuko, who is turning into a demon slowly. Tanjiro sets out to become a demon slayer to avenge his family and cure his sister.


I dont like watching sad stories, but this stories have overwhelmed me in many ways, the arts, the storylines and more. I can't help but keep coming back to watch this series.
